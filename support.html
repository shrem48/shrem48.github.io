EduFlash AI Support

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Need help with EduFlash AI?

Contact us at:
support@eduflashai.com

Please include the following when contacting support:

  • the email address linked to your EduFlash AI account;
  • your device model and operating system version;
  • the app version, if known;
  • a short description of the issue;
  • screenshots, if relevant.

1. Account and Login Issues

Contact us if you need help with:

  • email/password sign in problems;
  • Sign in with Apple issues;
  • Google Sign-In issues;
  • password or account access issues;
  • account-related questions.

2. Subscription and Premium Access

For subscription questions, contact support and include:

  • the platform used for purchase (Apple App Store or Google Play);
  • the approximate purchase date;
  • the issue you are experiencing.

Please note:

  • subscription billing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play;
  • cancellation and subscription management are completed through your platform account settings;
  • refund requests are subject to the policies of the platform through which you purchased.

3. Study Content and Technical Issues

Contact us if:

  • uploaded study items do not process correctly;
  • generated summaries, quiz questions, or flashcards fail to appear;
  • library items do not load as expected;
  • the app crashes or behaves unexpectedly.

4. Privacy Requests

To request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, email: support@eduflashai.com

Use the subject line: Privacy Request – EduFlash AI

We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

5. Response Scope

We aim to provide reasonable support for issues related to the EduFlash AI app and service. We may decline requests that are abusive, fraudulent, technically unsupported, or unrelated to the Service.
