Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

EduFlash AI (“EduFlash AI,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI-powered study assistant that helps users turn study notes, images, and educational content into summaries, quiz questions, flashcards, and other study materials.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and what choices you have when you use the EduFlash AI mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the EduFlash AI application, our related websites and support pages, and any interactions you have with us regarding the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

A. Account Information

When you create or use an account, we may collect:

  • your email address;
  • authentication-related information;
  • account identifiers associated with supported sign-in methods, including email/password, Sign in with Apple, and Google Sign-In;
  • account settings and language preferences.

We do not store your plain-text password. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing and security practices where password-based authentication is used.

B. User Content

When you use the Service, we may collect and store:

  • images you upload;
  • extracted text from your study materials;
  • titles, notes, summaries, quiz questions, flashcards, and other study content generated or saved through the Service;
  • saved items in your study library.

C. Subscription and Transaction Information

If you purchase a subscription, billing is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play, not by EduFlash AI directly. We may receive limited subscription-related information such as:

  • subscription status;
  • entitlement or premium access status;
  • transaction-related identifiers needed to validate premium access.

We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

D. Device and Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information necessary to operate, secure, and improve the Service, such as:

  • app version;
  • device type;
  • operating system version;
  • error logs and diagnostic information;
  • service performance information;
  • request metadata needed for security, reliability, and troubleshooting.

E. Communications

If you contact us for support, feedback, legal requests, or privacy requests, we collect the information you provide and any related correspondence.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • authenticate users and maintain sessions;
  • provide the core features of the Service;
  • process uploaded study materials and generate AI-based outputs;
  • save, organize, and display your study library content;
  • provide premium subscription access and restore purchases;
  • maintain security, detect abuse, and prevent fraud;
  • troubleshoot issues, monitor performance, and improve the Service;
  • respond to support, legal, and privacy requests;
  • comply with legal obligations.

4. AI Processing

EduFlash AI uses AI technologies to analyze uploaded study material and generate educational outputs such as summaries, quiz questions, and flashcards.

To provide these features, your submitted content may be processed by service providers that support our hosting, infrastructure, and AI functionality. You should not upload highly sensitive personal information, confidential records, or any content you are not authorized to use.

AI-generated content may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your specific academic needs. You are responsible for reviewing and verifying all outputs before relying on them.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • with service providers who help us operate the Service, including providers involved in hosting, authentication, storage, subscription validation, customer support, and AI processing;
  • with Apple, Google, or other platform providers as needed to operate sign-in, subscriptions, restore purchases, or other platform-based features;
  • if required by law, legal process, or governmental request;
  • to enforce our Terms of Service, protect our rights, prevent fraud, or address security issues;
  • in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or sale of assets, subject to applicable legal protections.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • provide the Service;
  • maintain your account and saved study content;
  • comply with legal, tax, accounting, and security obligations;
  • resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

If you delete your account or request deletion, we may delete or anonymize your information, except where retention is required by law or reasonably necessary for legitimate business, legal, or security purposes.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • access certain personal information we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate information;
  • request deletion of your personal information;
  • object to or request restriction of certain processing;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To make a privacy-related request, contact: support@eduflashai.com

8. Subscription and Platform Purchases

Subscriptions purchased through Apple App Store or Google Play are managed through the relevant platform. Cancellation, renewal settings, refunds, and billing controls are subject to the policies and tools of the platform through which you purchased.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission, storage, or processing is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s Privacy

EduFlash AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact: support@eduflashai.com

11. International Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred and processed as described in this Privacy Policy, subject to applicable law.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the “Last Updated” date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
